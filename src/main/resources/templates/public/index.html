VÖBB – Your Gateway to Berlin’s Knowledge and Culture

The Verbund der Öffentlichen Bibliotheken Berlins (VÖBB) is more than just a library network — it’s a vibrant cultural and educational hub serving the entire Berlin community. With over 80 branches across the city, VÖBB connects people to a world of ideas, inspiration, and information.

Our mission is to make learning, discovery, and cultural exchange accessible to all — regardless of age, background, or language. Whether you’re diving into a novel, exploring new research, picking up a new language, or attending one of our many events, VÖBB is your welcoming space to grow and connect.

VÖBB - What We Offer

  • A massive digital and physical collection — books, eBooks, films, music, and more
  • Free Wi-Fi, study spaces, and workstations in modern facilities
  • Events, workshops, and exhibitions for all ages
  • Multilingual resources and inclusive services for Berlin’s diverse communities
  • 24/7 digital access through our online portal and app
  • At VÖBB, we believe libraries are not just about books — they’re about people, stories, and possibilities.
